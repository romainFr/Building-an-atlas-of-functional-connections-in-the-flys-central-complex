It is important to note two things at this stage. First, non-overlapping pairs usually showed no fluctuations upon stimulation, and when they did, they were small and unreliable (see Figure \ref{489066}Di), likely reflecting indirect effects. This suggests that the effects observed with our stimulation protocol were likely the result of direct synaptic connections. Secondly, and not surprisingly, responses were always detected with same-cell-type-stimulation controls (see Figure \ref{489066}Dii).
